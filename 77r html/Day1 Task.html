Biography of Pawan Kalyan

Introduction:

Pawan Kalyan is a famous Indian Actor and Politician.He is known as thePower Star Of Telugu Cinema for his Powerful Performances and large fan base.

Early Life:

He was born on September 2,1971, in Bapatla,Andhra Pradesh. From Young age, he Showed interst in martial arts and earned a Black belt in Karate.

Career:

He Started his film career with Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi in 1996. He Became famous After Tholi prema and gave many
hit Movies Like Gabbar singh , Attarintiki Daredi and OG.

Political Life:

In 2014, he founded the Jana Sena Party to serve the people and bring positive change in society. He continues to be active
in politics and public service.Presently, he is the Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh

Conclusion:

Pawan Kalyan is admired for his talent, simplicity, and dedication.



                                    He is not only a successful actor but also an inspiring leader who motivates millions of fans to do good for society.
